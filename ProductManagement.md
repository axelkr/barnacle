# Barnacle
## Vision
Each part of an organization, be it business units, teams or individuals, can analyze the Kanban processes it's part of. Each part of an organization recognizes immediately its own possible improvements. 

## Mission
Integrate and display Kanban boards from several sources. Visualize Kanban metrics on all levels.
